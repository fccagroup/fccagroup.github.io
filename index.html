WE ARE FCCA

A political party.

We believe in a staunch loyalty to the values that formed our country, and returning those beliefs to the forefront of our society

Society

Everyone has their place in society, some lower and some higher than others. This feudal ladder is the hard-won product of eight hundred years of Great British patriotism, eight hundred long years of our glorious culture, conquering those that fail to climb.

Our other policies reflect our determination to return our country to its former glory. For one, we wish to raise taxes. But donâ€™t worry, for We will use the taxes to afford a reclamation of old, indecisive territories, and in doing so we will reboot our nascent trade industry, and, once we have achieved this, we will make sure we lower our taxes.

Besides this, we want to privatise the train service, and run our health service and other nationalised services as businesses. In doing this, we will boost our economic output by at least 1 percent! The achievement would be huge!

For many years, we were defined by our empire, and, in that way, we defined the world. We hope to return Britain, and its latent empire- to its former glory, firstly by reinstating a system of hard feudalism, but also by welcoming prevalent capitalism back into our poor, wounded nation, and reclaiming our hard-won society from those that seek to put an end to our economy, and our dying empire - for good.
